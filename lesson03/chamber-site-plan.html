Overview

Purpose

The Ancud Chamber of Commerce is a membership organization that serves as an economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We aim to build a more prosperous community through economic and workforce development, new business connections, meaningful community partnerships, and support and advocacy for small businesses. We promote business and entrepreneurship by providing member services, fostering economic and community development, and advocating for policies that balance economic wellness.

Scenarios

- What are the advantages and services that the chamber offers its members and how can you benefit from them? - What current events and programs the chamber is hosting and how can you participate? - How can you promote your business and increase your visibility on the Chamber's website, directory, newsletter, social media and other channels? - How can you get involved and volunteer for chamber committees, groups or projects?
